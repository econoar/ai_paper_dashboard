About This Dashboard

Welcome to the AI Research Papers Dashboard! This site is designed to help you quickly ingest, review, and share the latest research papers in artificial intelligence and machine learning.

How It Works

Broad Filtering:
Our dashboard queries the arXiv database to retrieve papers from three broad categories:

  • cs.AI – Artificial Intelligence
  • cs.LG – Machine Learning
  • stat.ML – Statistical Machine Learning

Secondary Keyword Filtering:
After retrieving papers from these categories, our system further checks each paper's title and abstract for specific keywords such as “reinforcement learning,” “digital twin,” “agent coordination,” and others. If none of these terms are detected, the Tags column will display "N/A." This simply means that none of your preset keywords were found in the paper—even though it still belongs to one of the broad categories.

Key Features

  • Paper Listings: Papers are grouped by publication day, showing key details such as time (PST), detected keywords (or “N/A”), the title, and an abstract excerpt.
  • More Info: Click the "More Info" button to view detailed paper information in a modal window.
  • Summarize: Generate an AI-crafted summary in a customizable modal. You can adjust options such as minimum and maximum token lengths, choose from different summarization models, and even edit a default prompt. The summary is tailored for sharing on social media.
  • Bookmark: Save papers of interest to your bookmarks. Bookmarked papers are stored locally in your browser, and you can view them on a dedicated Bookmarks page.
  • Social Sharing: Once a summary is generated, you can share the paper's title, summary, and a PDF link directly to Twitter.
  • Refresh & Search: A manual refresh button lets you update the list on demand while an integrated autocomplete search bar helps you quickly find papers by title.
