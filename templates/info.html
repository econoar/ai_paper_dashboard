About This Dashboard

Welcome to the AI Research Papers Dashboard! This site is designed to help you quickly ingest, review, and share the latest research papers in artificial intelligence and machine learning.

How It Works

Broad Filtering:
Our dashboard queries the arXiv database for papers in three broad categories – cs.AI, cs.LG, and stat.ML.

Secondary Keyword Filtering:
After retrieving papers from these categories, our system checks each paper’s title and abstract for specific keywords such as “reinforcement learning,” “digital twin,” “agent coordination,” etc. If none are detected, the Tags column displays "N/A."

Key Features

  • Paper Listings: Papers are grouped by publication day and show details such as detected keywords (or “N/A”), title, and abstract excerpt.
  • More Info: Click the "More Info" button to view detailed information in a modal.
  • Summarize: Generate an AI-crafted summary with customizable options, designed for social media sharing.
  • Bookmark: Save papers of interest for later review on the Bookmarks page.
  • Social Sharing: Share a paper’s title, summary, and PDF link directly to Twitter, Telegram, or LinkedIn.
  • Refresh & Search: Use the manual refresh button and an autocomplete search bar to quickly navigate the papers.
